Working papers

1. Bank Credit Response to Corporate Disclosure Regulation

  - Using supervisory bank-borrower level data from the Deutsche Bundesbank, the question to be explored in this study is whether and how bank credit responds to corporate disclosure regulation.

2. Spillover Effects of Corporate Disclosure Regulation: Evidence from German Firms and Counties

  - Using proprietary datasets from the Deutsche Bundesbank, in this study I investigate the impact of private firm disclosure mandates on the performance of local firms and local macroeconomic outcomes.
